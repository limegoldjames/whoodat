How To Play

A famous face appears on screen — who is it? You start with 1,000 points. Guess correctly to score big. Buy hints to help — but they'll cost you!

❌ Wrong Guess −50 points per wrong answer.
💡 Buy a Hint Unlock clues: nationality, era, occupation & more.
⏭ Skip Skip a person at the cost of all remaining points for that round.
